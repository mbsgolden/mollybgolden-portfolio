About Me

I am inspired by the designs that shape how we move through and interact with the world, whether that be the ecological designs that shape our landscape or human designs that shape our experience in our community, in our homes, and on the web. I believe good design can foster connectedness, share stories that reflect on who we are, and foster beauty in the world, including the "www". I strive to create beautiful and functional web designs that allow clients to share their story with the world.
